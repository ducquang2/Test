Walter O'Brien

A high IQ child prodigy who started programming computers at age nine

Here's a time line of Walter O'Brien's life:

  • 1975 "- Born in Clonroche, County Wexford, Ireland."
  • 1984 "- O'Brien's father gave him livestock as payment for doing chores on the farm, which he sold in order to purchase an Amstrad personal computer at age nine."
  • 1988 "- He hacked into NASA under the pseudonym "Scorpion"."
  • 1993 "O'Brien was a member of the Irish team that participated in the 1993 International Olympiad in Informatics computer coding competition."
  • 2014 "O'Brien described his company as a think tank for "high IQ individuals"."
